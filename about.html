our history

Powering a Greener Tomorrow, One Drop at a Time

Kamakhya Biofuels Pvt. Ltd. is a forward-looking enterprise dedicated to advancing India's clean energy mission through the production of grain-based ethanol. Inspired by the Government of India’s Ethanol Blending in Petrol (EBP) program, KBPL aims to reduce dependency on fossil fuels while boosting the rural economy through sustainable practices and job creation. With a strategic location in Bediasisa Gaon, Darrang, Assam, KBPL is poised to become a key player in the renewable energy sector in Northeast India.

Company started

The company is on track to commence commercial production by September 2024, marking a significant milestone in its operational journey.

Kamakhya Biofuels Pvt. Ltd. is a company dedicated to the production of ethanol from grain, established under the Government of India’s Ethanol Blended Petrol (EBP) program. Our facility is located in Bediasisa Gaon, under the Sipajhar revenue circle in Darrang district, Assam.

KBPL was incorporated on September 14, 2021. Since then, we have been working diligently to set up our ethanol production facility.

Our ethanol manufacturing plant is designed to produce 300 KLPD (Kilo Liters Per Day) of ethanol. This capacity is aligned with the requirements of the EBP program and aims to contribute to the renewable energy sector.

KBPL has a 6.0 MW co-generation power plant. This power generation will utilize high-pressure steam from a 55 TPH boiler, which will provide steam for both the production process and power generation through a steam turbine generator.

To comply with environmental regulations, we will install an Electrostatic Precipitator (ESP) as per Assam Pollution Control Board (APCB) norms. Additionally, our facility will feature a 65-meter-high chimney to ensure proper emission dispersion.
